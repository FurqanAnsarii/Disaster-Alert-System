# 📌 Executive Summary

Natural disasters pose significant threats to human life, infrastructure, and economic stability. Traditional disaster reporting systems often suffer from delayed communication and fragmented response mechanisms.

The **Real-Time Disaster Alert System** is an intelligent Java-based platform engineered to provide rapid disaster detection, alert dissemination, emergency protocol management, and historical incident tracking through a centralized monitoring architecture.

The platform demonstrates advanced Object-Oriented Design principles, scalable software architecture, and real-world emergency management workflows.
